Try it out

To initiate a Hangout using the Hangout Moderator app, click the green button below. From within that Hangout, the tester should then invite 4 additional participants to join in the conversation. The first person to join the Hangout and open the Hangout Moderator app is automatically designated as the instructor. All participants who join after will be students. Be sure to instruct all participants who join to open the Hangout Moderator app, because it may not load by default.
